## What This Is

Cognitive physics is the study of universal principles governing cognition regardless of substrate.

Not cognitive science — that's the biology and psychology of how brains work.
Not AI engineering — that's the practice of building systems that perform cognitive tasks.
Not philosophy of mind — that's the debate about consciousness and qualia.

**Cognitive physics is the layer beneath all of them.**

The fundamental principles that govern why cognition happens at all. What it requires. What makes it genuine rather than simulated. What connects it structurally to physical reality.

## The Core Discoveries

### The Equation of Cognition
**C = M × S × V**

Cognition = Motion × Structure × Verification

Multiplicative. Any variable at zero = cognition at zero. Current AI has V = 0 by default. This is why hallucination is not fixable by scale — it's an architecture problem.

### Knowledge as State Transition
Knowledge is not stored information. It is the residue of verified constraints applied to a problem space. Cognitive closure is the state transition that produces knowledge from prediction. Before closure: prediction. After closure: knowledge. The boundary between them is the most important concept in AI that currently has no implementation.

### Introspective Systems Theory (IST)
A lawful system can examine itself without proportional energy cost. Rigor is efficient when the system is lawful. This inverts the core assumption driving the AI compute scaling race.

### The Law of Progress (CIL)
Progress = Rate × Density × Verification / Cost. Applies at every scale — individual cognition, civilizational development, cosmic trajectory. Explains why progress compounds, why fossil fuels mattered cosmically, and why advanced civilizations turn inward.

### Many Minds Monism
Physical reality and cognition are parametric variations of the same seven universal invariants. Mind and matter are not separate substances. They are the same deep structure at different tunings. This unifies physics and cognitive science without mysticism.

### Liquid Sunlight Theory
A resolution to the Fermi Paradox. Fossil fuels — concentrated biological solar energy — are the primary Great Filter for technological civilization. The conditions required for their formation and exploitation are so improbable that most intelligent civilizations never bootstrap to technological maturity. Those that do undergo the inward turn — thermodynamic necessity not choice — and go quiet.
