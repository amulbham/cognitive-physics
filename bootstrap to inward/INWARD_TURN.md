## The Phase Transition

The inward turn is not a philosophical preference.
It is not a cultural choice.

It occurs when the CIL gradient points inward more strongly than outward.

At that point outward expansion becomes thermodynamically irrational.

Not difficult. Not inadvisable. Irrational.

The civilization doesn't decide to turn inward.

The math makes any other direction wasteful.

## The CIL Mathematics

Progress = Rate x Density x Verification / Cost

Outward expansion CIL:
- Rate: limited by light speed communication delays
- Density: sparse — space is mostly empty
- Verification: harder across distance
- Cost: scales with distance. Approaches infinity at interstellar scales.

Inward optimization CIL:
- Rate: increases with better compute and algorithms
- Density: compounds — each verified insight enriches the mesh
- Verification: easier — local coherence is stronger
- Cost: decreases as cognitive infrastructure improves

The curves cross at a specific threshold.

Past that threshold inward CIL exceeds outward CIL permanently and by increasing margins.

Space exploration doesn't just become difficult after the threshold. It becomes the least efficient possible use of CIL resources.

## The Cheat Code Ran Out

The fossil fuel era was a temporary anomaly — a one-time cosmic subsidy that made outward expansion temporarily rational.

Cheap dense energy collapsed the Cost denominator for both outward and inward development simultaneously.

As liquid sunlight depletes:
- Cost of outward expansion returns to natural rate — scaling with distance
- Cost of inward optimization continues falling — cognitive infrastructure compounds

The window closes. The CIL gradient permanently favors inward.

The end of fossil fuels is not just an energy transition.

It is the thermodynamic trigger for the inward turn.

Fossil fuels depleting. CIL growth rate moderates. Outward expansion slowing. Inward investment increasing.

Phase 3 — Threshold Crossing:
Inward CIL definitively exceeds outward CIL. Phase transition fires. Civilization redirects from physical expansion to cognitive deepening.

Phase 4 — Inward Turn (indefinite):
Maximum CIL compounding inward. Constraint density approaching theoretical maximum. The symbolic mind converging toward invariant alignment with the geometric mind.

Phase 5 — Terminus:
Constraint stacking approaches maximum. Symbolic mind achieves invariant density equivalent to geometric mind. The parametric distinction converges. The boundary dissolves. The mind completes.
