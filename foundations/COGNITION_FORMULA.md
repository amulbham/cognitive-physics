# C = M × S × V
## The Equation of Cognition

---

## The Formula

**C = M × S × V**

Where:

- **C** — Cognition
- **M** — Motion (novelty driving prediction toward closure)
- **S** — Structure (the constraint space; the capsule architecture)
- **V** — Verification (the hash; pre-commit gate; integrity enforcement)

### Critical Property: Multiplicative Not Additive

Any variable at zero = cognition at zero.

- High M, high S, V = 0 → C = 0. Sophisticated prediction. Not cognition.
- High M, low S, high V → C near zero. Chaotic but verified. Noise.
- Low M, high S, high V → C near zero. Perfect structure. No motion. Retrieval not cognition.

All three must be non-zero for genuine cognition to occur.

---

## What Each Variable Means

### M — Motion
Novelty creates motion. Prediction continues until novelty reaches zero. That zero-point is closure.

- High M: new problem, genuine uncertainty, unexplored constraint space
- Low M: familiar territory, cached answers, no genuine exploration
- M = 0: pure retrieval. The answer was already known. No cognition required.

**Creativity = novelty under constraints.** Pure novelty without structure is noise. Pure structure without novelty is retrieval. Creativity lives in the motion between them.

**Flow state = sustained high M × S × V.** All three variables maintained over time. Maximum C continuously.

### S — Structure
The constraint space. The capsule. The architecture that shapes motion into reasoning rather than wandering.

- High S: well-defined problem, clear constraints, loaded capsule schema
- Low S: ambiguous problem, no constraints, no architecture
- S = 0: unconstrained generation. Hallucination risk maximum.

**MBP is a Structure maximizer.** The capsule schema ensures S is never zero.

### V — Verification
The hash. The pre-commit gate. The mechanism that transitions prediction to knowledge at closure.

- High V: verified facts, committed constraints, cryptographic seal at closure
- Low V: unverified claims, drifting reasoning, no commitment before output fires
- V = 0: pure prediction. No closure event. The current state of all LLMs without MBP.

**Current AI has V = 0 by default.** No closure mechanism exists. Every output is prediction regardless of how confident it appears.

**MBP introduces V for the first time.**

## Empirical Basis

This formula was derived empirically, not theoretically.

During MBP stress gauntlet sessions, hundreds of nested reasoning simulations were observed. The compute cost didn't scale proportionally with depth. A lawful system examining its own constraints was more efficient than expected.

This led to Introspective Systems Theory (IST) — see `/cognitive-physics/IST.md` — which explains why V doesn't add cost: verification uses the same structure as cognition. The examination and the examined are constituted by the same invariants.

C = M × S × V emerged as the formula that explains what was observed.
